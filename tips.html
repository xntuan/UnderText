üíæSaving Sentences to Flashcards

Build your vocabulary by saving translations as you chat!

  1. Type your message in the keyboard
  2. See the translation appear at the top of the keyboard
  3. Tap on any word(s) in the translation to select them
  4. Tap the ‚≠ê Star icon (Learn button) that appears
  5. The entire sentence pair is now saved to your flashcards!

üí° Pro Tip

UnderText always saves the complete sentence pair (your full input + full translation), not individual words. This is perfect for learning phrases in context and seeing how words are used naturally!

üìöAccessing Your Flashcards

Review your saved vocabulary anytime:

  1. Open the UnderText main app (not the keyboard)
  2. Tap on "Flashcards" tab at the bottom (star icon)
  3. You'll see all your saved sentence pairs organized by language
  4. Tap "Review" in the top-right corner to start studying
  5. Tap cards to flip and see the translation
  6. Rate your memory: Again, Hard, Good, or Easy

üí° Spaced Repetition

UnderText uses an intelligent review system. Cards you find difficult will appear more frequently, while easy cards appear less often. This maximizes your learning efficiency!

  3. The full sentence translates back to your original language
  4. Compare it with what you originally typed to verify accuracy

üîÄLanguage Selection & Quick Switch

Control which languages you're translating between!

Setting Your Main Languages:

  1. Open the UnderText main app
  2. Tap the Settings tab at the bottom
  3. Find the "Languages" section at the top
  4. Choose "From" (your input/typing language)
  5. Choose "To" (the language you're learning)
  6. Tap the swap button (‚Üï) to quickly reverse direction

Quick Language Switch (Advanced):

Switch languages while typing without leaving the keyboard!

  1. In the main app, enable "Quick Language Switch" toggle
  2. Configure your switch codes (default: vnn = Vietnamese, enn = English, etc.)
  3. While typing, type the code (like vnn) to switch to that language
  4. Visual feedback appears showing your current language
  5. The keyboard layout and Telex automatically adjust

üí° Best Learning Method

Daily Practice:

  1. Use the keyboard in your real conversations (WhatsApp, Messages, etc.)
  2. Type naturally, even if you make mistakes
  3. Watch the real-time translation as you type
  4. Save interesting words to flashcards
  5. Review flashcards before bed (spaced repetition works!)
  6. Use back translation to verify meanings

Result: You learn vocabulary in context, which is 10x more effective than memorizing lists!
