Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Your privacy is our top priority.

1. Data Collection

We collect ZERO data.

UnderText does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Keystrokes or typed text
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or messages

2. Full Access Permission

The keyboard requires "Full Access" permission for the following technical reasons:

  • Real-time Translation: To process and translate text as you type
  • Translation Button: To send selected text to translation services
  • AI Services: To communicate with third-party translation APIs
  • Voice Input: To transcribe audio using Whisper API

Important: Even with Full Access enabled, we guarantee that we do not track, record, or transmit your keystrokes or any typed content beyond what you explicitly request to translate.

3. Translation Services

UnderText offers multiple translation options:

Apple Translation (On-Device)

  • Privacy: 100% on-device processing
  • No Data Sent: Nothing leaves your phone
  • Offline Capable: Works without internet
  • Recommended: Most private option

Third-Party AI Services (Optional)

If you choose to use third-party translation services (Microsoft Translator, OpenAI, Google Gemini, DeepSeek), please note:

  • Only Translated Text is Sent: Only the text you explicitly select and choose to translate is sent to these services
  • Your API Key: You provide your own API key, giving you direct control
  • Third-Party Privacy Policies Apply: When using these services, their privacy policies govern how they handle your data
  • No UnderText Servers: We do not route your data through our servers. Communication goes directly from your device to the service provider

4. API Key Storage

If you configure third-party translation services:

  • Secure Storage: API keys are stored locally in iOS Keychain (encrypted)
  • Never Transmitted to Us: Keys never leave your device or get sent to LanguageKeyboard servers
  • Your Control: You can delete API keys anytime from Settings

5. Subscription and Payments

All subscription and payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store:

  • Apple Handles Payments: We use StoreKit 2 for subscription management
  • No Payment Data Access: We do not have access to your payment information
  • Apple's Privacy Policy: Apple's privacy policy governs subscription data

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data in the first place.

7. Children's Privacy

UnderText does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. We do not collect data from users of any age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Third-Party Services Privacy Policies

If you choose to use third-party translation services, please review their privacy policies:

  • Microsoft Translator: Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • OpenAI: OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: Google Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek: DeepSeek Privacy Policy
