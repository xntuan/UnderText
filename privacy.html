Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our commitment: UnderText is built with privacy as our foundation. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Period.
System Requirement: UnderText requires iOS 26.0 or later to function properly.

1. What Data We Collect

We collect ZERO data.

UnderText does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • ❌ Keystrokes or anything you type
  • ❌ Personal information
  • ❌ Analytics or usage data
  • ❌ Device information
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contact lists or photos
  • ❌ Any telemetry or tracking data

2. What We Store Locally (On Your Device Only)

The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • ✅ App settings (language preferences, keyboard layout)
  • ✅ Vocabulary flashcards (optional feature)
  • ✅ Daily translation quota for free users (5 translations/day)
  • ✅ Premium subscription status (managed by Apple's StoreKit)

3. Full Access Permission

iOS requires keyboard apps to request "Full Access" permission for certain features. Here's exactly what we use it for:

What Full Access Enables:

  • Network access for Azure OpenAI translation - Only if you configure your own API key (optional)
  • On-device Apple Translation - Completely private, processed locally on your device (iOS 26.0+)
  • Speech synthesis - For word pronunciation feature
  • Dictionary API access - For word definition lookups

What We DO NOT Do:

  • ❌ NO keystroke tracking or logging
  • ❌ NO data collection or analytics
  • ❌ NO third-party data sharing (except your chosen Azure API if configured)
  • ❌ NO advertisements
  • ❌ NO selling your data

Important: Even with Full Access enabled, we do not track, log, or store anything you type. The permission is solely for the features listed above.

4. Translation Services

UnderText offers exactly 2 translation options. You choose which one to use:

Option 1: Apple Translation (Default & Recommended)

  • ✅ Completely private and on-device (iOS 26.0+)
  • ✅ Zero data transmission
  • ✅ Apple does not collect or see your typed text
  • ✅ Works offline after language models are downloaded
  • ✅ Available to all users (free tier: 5 translations/day, Premium: more translations)
  • ✅ Always available as fallback (even when Premium tokens run out)

Option 2: Azure OpenAI GPT-4o-mini (Optional, Premium Only)

  • ⚙️ Server-based AI translation service
  • ⚙️ Requests go through our secure server
  • ⚙️ No API keys required from you
  • ⚙️ Premium subscription required
  • ⚙️ Token-based daily quota

5. Subscription and Payments

All subscription and payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through StoreKit 2:

  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • We do not store credit card details
  • Apple's privacy policy governs all transactions
  • Your subscription status is verified locally through Apple's APIs

Pricing:

  • Monthly: $3/month (no trial, immediate payment)
  • Yearly: $24/year (3-day FREE trial, save 33%)

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

The only external connection is if you use Azure OpenAI translation (Premium only) - in which case, translation requests go through our secure server which validates your subscription before processing.

7. Children's Privacy

UnderText does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Our app is safe for all ages.
