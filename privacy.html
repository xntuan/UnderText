2. Data We Collect

2.1 Translation Data (Premium Feature Only)

When you use Azure AI translation (Premium subscription only), we transmit the following data to our secure Azure backend:

  • Text for Translation: The specific text you choose to translate (not all your keystrokes)
  • Language Pair: Source and target languages you select
  • Anonymous Device Identifier: A random UUID to validate your subscription status
  • App Version: For technical support and compatibility

How We Use This Data:

  • To provide real-time AI translation service
  • To validate your Premium subscription status
  • To ensure service quality and prevent abuse

Data Retention: Translation requests are processed in real-time and not permanently stored. Server logs may retain anonymous usage metrics (request count, timestamp) for up to 30 days for system maintenance.

2.2 Data Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

The following data never leaves your device:

  • App settings (language preferences, keyboard layouts)
  • Vocabulary flashcards (if you create them)
  • Free translation quota tracking (5 translations/day for free users)
  • Premium subscription status (managed by Apple StoreKit)

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your keystrokes (except text you explicitly choose to translate)
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other device content
  • Browsing history or app usage outside UnderText
  • Biometric data
  • Financial information (payments handled by Apple)

4. How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Anonymization: Device identifiers are randomized UUIDs, not linked to your personal identity
  • No Tracking: We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels
  • Secure Infrastructure: Azure backend follows industry-standard security practices

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Azure OpenAI (Premium Feature)

Translation requests use Azure OpenAI API. Microsoft's privacy policy applies to data processed by their services. Learn more: Microsoft Privacy Statement

5.2 Apple Services

  • Apple Translation: Free tier uses Apple's on-device Translation framework (data never leaves your device)
  • StoreKit: Premium subscriptions managed by Apple (Apple's privacy policy applies)

5.3 Free Dictionary API

English word definitions use Free Dictionary API (open-source, no data retention). Learn more: Free Dictionary API

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing: Data is only shared with:

  • Azure OpenAI (for translation processing only)
  • Apple (for subscription management only)

7. Your Rights

7.1 Data Access & Deletion

  • Translation Data: Not permanently stored; requests processed in real-time
  • Local Data: Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Server Logs: Contact us to request deletion of any server-side logs associated with your device ID

7.2 Opt-Out

  • Use Free tier (Apple Translation) to avoid sending data to our servers
  • Disable "Allow Full Access" to use keyboard without translation features
  • Cancel Premium subscription anytime through Apple subscriptions

8. Children's Privacy

UnderText is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us immediately.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us at: support@undertext.app

Summary: UnderText collects minimal data (only translation text and anonymous device ID) when you use Premium AI translation. Free users transmit no data to our servers. We never collect personal information, track your behavior, or sell your data.
